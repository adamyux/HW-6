Welcome to play Mad Libs!

Game Rule

  • One player acts as the “reader” and asks the other players, who haven't seen the story, to fill in the blanks with adjectives, nouns, exclamations, colors, adjectives, and more.
  • These words are inserted into the blanks and then the story is read aloud to hilarious results. There are no winners or losers, only laughter.

Welcome to play Rock Paper Scissors!

Game Rule

  • If one player picks rock and the other paper, the player who showed rock succeeds.
  • If one player picks paper and the other paper, then it's a tie.
  • If one player picks scissors and the other paper, the player who showed scissors succeeds.
